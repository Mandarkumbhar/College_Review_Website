   Bachelor of Technology [B.Tech] (Electrical Engineering)

      Stream : ENGINEERING
      Duration : 4 YEARS
      Course ELECTRICAL
      Course Type : GRADUATION  

            B.Tech. Electrical Engineering is a 4 years degree course. The eligibility of admission in this course is 10+2 pass or equivalent from a recognized board or university. Electrical engineering is one of the most sought-after branches of engineering. Admission to this course is done through either entrance exams conducted at national and state level or on merit based on the qualifying exam.
            Top Institutes offering this course are:
            IIT, Guwahati
            NIT, Thiruchirapalli
            The Neotia University
            Chandigarh University
            NIT, Warangal
            Average fees for this course in most of the engineering colleges in India vary from INR 4 to 10 Lacs
Electrical Engineers have job opportunities in both private and public sectors. Shell, TATA technologies, DRDO, Dr. Reddy’s Laboratories, HCL technologies, Google, Microsoft, Power grid corporation of India Ltd, Samsung, Sony Corporation etc. are some of the most renowned recruiters of this field. Average salary package offered to Electrical Engineers is 3.5 to 6 LPA in most of the top recruiting companies.
Electrical engineering is the study and application of electricity, electronics a, d electromagnetism for the development and maintenance of electrical and electronics equipment. Electrical Engineering course study includes Circuit analysis, machines and drive system, Power System, Control system, Power electronics, Utilization etc. The main focus of the course will be on industrial requirement of ‘Electrical engineering’.

B.Tech. Electrical Engineering: What is it About?

            The scope for students of B. Tech Electrical Engineering is very high. There are two options available for them either they can join higher education after this course or they can join their professional career as per skills. At present, electrical engineers have great demand in the market.
            An objective of this course is to ensure the success of the students for a long time. Students can develop their electrical skills and abilities with the study of this course. They also can improve abilities of professional attitude, effective communication skills as well.

B.Tech. Electrical Engineering: Eligibility

            Pass in 10+2 or equivalent with Physics, Mathematics and English as compulsory subjects with a minimum of 50% marks from a recognized board/ university. Students are expected to clear entrance exam like IIT, AIEEE, AMU, UPSEE, BITSAT etc.

B.Tech. Electrical Engineering: Admission Process


            To get admission in engineering colleges, first of all student should be 10+2 with physics, chemistry and mathematics. After that the student can attempt entrance exam or they can get direct admission also.
            After clearing the entrance exam, counselling will be done and the eligible candidate will be selected. And, if the student is getting direct admission then selection will be done on the basis of merit list or management quota.
            Following is the criteria for admission through different modes-
            Direct Admission- Engineering institutes which offer direct admission have certain criteria for admission. Either they offer admission on the basis of merit in qualifying exam i.e. 10+2 or equivalent or there are some management seats reserved in private engineering colleges. For management quota the fee structure is generally higher than others.
            Direct Admission for Diploma holders- Various engineering colleges in India also offer direct admission to ITI diploma holders in second year of the course.
            Entrance Exams- There are several entrance exams in India at national and state level. Some private institutes like SRM University, BITS Pilani, VIT University etc conduct their separate entrance test.
